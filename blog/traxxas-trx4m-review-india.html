Traxxas TRX-4M Review India: Mini Crawler That Punches Above Its Weight

RC
RC & Diecast Team
February 2025

The Traxxas TRX-4M arrived in India with considerable fanfare — and for good reason. As a 1/18th scale rock crawler from one of RC's most trusted brands, it promised to bring genuine Traxxas engineering to a price point accessible to the Indian hobbyist market. After putting it through extensive testing on Mumbai's rocky outskirts and Delhi's urban obstacle courses, here's our full verdict.

"The TRX-4M is genuinely impressive. It's not a toy — it's a miniature engineering exercise that happens to be a lot of fun."

Performance on Indian Terrain

Indian terrain is uniquely challenging for RC crawlers — loose red dirt, rocky outcrops, and urban concrete surfaces all present different demands. The TRX-4M handles all of them surprisingly well.

The portal axles are the star of the show. They increase ground clearance significantly and allow the TRX-4M to navigate obstacles that would beach similar-sized machines. On the rocky patches near Mumbai's Sanjay Gandhi National Park, the mini crawler climbed angles we expected to defeat it.

Steering response is crisp and proportional. This is one area where buying Traxxas over cheaper alternatives pays off immediately — the TQ radio system and servo tuning are noticeably better than budget alternatives in this price range.

Battery Life — The One Weak Point

Our biggest criticism is runtime. The included 1200mAh 1S LiPo delivers approximately 20–25 minutes of crawling before needing a charge. For a casual session, this is adequate, but serious enthusiasts will quickly invest in a second or third battery pack.

The good news: TRX-4M compatible batteries are now more widely available in India, and third-party 1500mAh options are easy to find online for under ₹500.

Who Should Buy the Traxxas TRX-4M?

  • Beginners who want a reliable, ready-to-run experience without mechanical complexity
  • Apartment dwellers who want an indoor/outdoor crawler that doesn't need a large space
  • Families looking for a premium RC experience that will survive enthusiastic use
  • Experienced hobbyists who want a capable second vehicle that's different from their full-size rigs

Verdict

The Traxxas TRX-4M is the easiest recommendation we've made in this category. It's not the cheapest mini crawler, but it's far and away the best value when you factor in build quality, performance, and the peace of mind that comes with Traxxas's track record.

If you're on the fence about getting into RC rock crawling, this is the perfect gateway drug. It's capable enough to surprise you, simple enough not to overwhelm, and fun enough to keep you coming back for more.
