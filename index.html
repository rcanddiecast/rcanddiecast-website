Land Rover
Camel Trophy Edition RTR
The Camel Trophy was a vehicle-oriented adventure event held between 1980 and 2000. Unlike typical races, its focus wasn't on speed but on testing the endurance of participants and vehicles in extreme conditions. Emphasizing teamwork and resilience, it earned the nickname 'The Olympics of 4x4.' As the core sponsor, Land Rover featured vehicles like the Range Rover, Defender 90, Defender 110, and Discovery. These iconic Land Rovers, painted in distinctive yellow sand, navigated some of the world's most challenging terrains, creating legendary off-road stories.
